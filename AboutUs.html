Our Stories

In 2019 Eagle was founded with a simple mission:
build the designs you’d use every day if it existed.

Growing up, my friend and I always had a passion for the designing. All new Furnished Furniture, Alluminium, PVC to interior designing shows with our teacher-turned-entrepreneur-father. We were raised with an appreciation for unique, lastest , and high-quality gear.

But the designs available at the time just weren’t up to our well settled uniqueness – the handles would break, the latches would snap off, and the lids would cave in. Not only was it a hassle to replace our design after every year, but also these cheaply built, ordinary ice chests were limiting our good times. And that frustration led us to a solution.

In 2019, we founded Eagle with a simple mission: build the design we’d use every day if it existed. One that was built for the extreme design's enthusiast rather than for the mass-discount retailers. One that could take the abuse we knew we’d put it through out in the field and on the water. One that simply wouldn’t break.

We decided early on that product innovation would come from necessity and firsthand experience – not from market research and data analysis. Today, Eagle products perform when it matters most.
